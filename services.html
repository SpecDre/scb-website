Services

Cut, core, break, dig, haul

Seven services, all run with our own crews and equipment out of one yard in Anaheim. Take one of them or take the whole package on a single contract.

01

Concrete Cutting

Clean, precise cuts in slab, wall and deck. Controlled slurry, square edges, and minimal impact on what's staying.

  • Flat sawing — slabs, roadways, trenches
  • Wall sawing — openings, door and window cuts
  • Wire sawing — thick sections and odd geometry
  • Hand sawing and curb cutting
  • Max depth of cut: 24 in. wall saw, 16 in. flat saw, 8 in. hand saw
02

Core Drilling

Cored openings for plumbing, electrical, HVAC, anchors and dowels — including overhead and angled work.

  • Slab and wall coring
  • Overhead coring
  • Angle and deep coring
  • Dowel holes and anchor sets
  • Diameters: 1/4 in. through 60 in.
03

Asphalt & Concrete Grinding

Skid steer mounted grinders for asphalt and concrete — trip hazards, high spots, transitions and surface prep, without tearing out and repaving.

  • Asphalt grinding and milling
  • Trip hazard removal
  • High spot and transition grinding
  • Surface prep before overlay
  • Parking lot and driveway grinding
  • ADA transition corrections
  • 24 in. grinding width, up to 6 in. depth per pass
04

Demolition & Breaking

Selective interior demo and structural breaking, sized to the space, the schedule and what has to stay standing.

  • Selective interior demolition
  • Compressor crews and jackhammers
  • Breaking and busting
  • Skid steer mounted breakers
  • Structural removal
05

Excavation & Grading

Dirt work with Bobcat skid steers and mini excavators, built for tight urban footprints and interior access.

  • Trenching and footings
  • Grading and site prep
  • Auger and post holes
  • Mini excavator work
  • Backfill and compaction
06

Hauling & Removal

Our own trucks, so the material leaves with the crew that made it instead of waiting on somebody else's schedule.

  • Concrete and asphalt removal
  • Dirt and spoils hauling
  • Dump truck service
  • Site cleanup and sweeping
07

Labor & Equipment

Crews and operated iron on time and material when the job needs hands and machines rather than a fixed scope.

  • Skilled labor crews
  • Operated equipment
  • Grinder and sweeper attachments
  • Loader and breaker work
  • Augers and specialty attachments

Emergency & off-hours

In a bind? Call us.

Schedules slip, inspections fail, something gets found behind a wall that nobody planned for. When you need a saw on site tonight instead of next Tuesday, call the office. We'd rather move a crew than watch you lose a week.

Nights and weekends

Occupied buildings, active retail, live hospitals and tight schedules often can't take daytime work. We run off-hours regularly — it isn't a special favor.

Emergency call-outs

Broken line, failed inspection, something uncovered mid-demo. Call and tell us what happened. After hours the phone goes to dispatch.

Short-notice mobilization

Our equipment sits in our own yard in Anaheim, not at a rental house. That's the difference between "we can be there" and "let me check availability."
