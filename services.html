Services

Cut, core, break, dig, haul

Seven services, all run with our own crews and equipment out of one yard. Take one of them or take the whole package on a single contract.

01

Concrete Cutting

Clean, precise cuts in slab, wall and deck. Controlled slurry, square edges, and minimal impact on what's staying.

  • Flat sawing — slabs, roadways, trenches
  • Wall sawing — openings, door and window cuts
  • Wire sawing — thick sections and odd geometry
  • Hand sawing and curb cutting
  • Max depth of cut: 24 in. wall saw, 16 in. flat saw, 8 in. hand saw
02

Core Drilling

Cored openings for plumbing, electrical, HVAC, anchors and dowels — including overhead and angled work.

  • Slab and wall coring
  • Overhead coring
  • Angle and deep coring
  • Dowel holes and anchor sets
  • Diameters: 1/4 in. through 60 in.
03

Asphalt & Concrete Grinding

Skid steer mounted asphalt planers for asphalt and concrete — trip hazards, high spots, transitions and surface prep, without tearing out and repaving.

  • Asphalt planing and cold milling
  • Trip hazard removal
  • High spot and transition grinding
  • Surface prep and scarifying before overlay
  • Parking lot and driveway planing
  • ADA transition corrections
  • Planer cuts to grade with adjustable depth and side shift
  • 24 in. planer width, up to 6 in. depth per pass
04

Demolition & Breaking

Selective interior demo and structural breaking, sized to the space, the schedule and what has to stay standing. Occupied buildings, tight access and off-hours work are normal for us.

  • Selective interior demolition
  • Tenant improvement and remodel demo
  • Slab, wall, stair and footing removal
  • Compressor crews with jackhammers and chipping guns
  • Hand demolition where machines won't fit
  • Breaking and busting
  • Skid steer mounted breakers
  • Structural removal
  • Debris hauled off by our own trucks
05

Excavation & Grading

Dirt work with Bobcat skid steers and mini excavators, built for tight urban footprints and interior access.

  • Trenching and footings
  • Grading and site prep
  • Auger and post holes
  • Mini excavator and backhoe work
  • Backfill and compaction
06

Hauling & Removal

Our own trucks, so the material leaves with our crew.

  • Concrete and asphalt removal
  • Dirt and spoils hauling
  • Dump truck service
  • Site cleanup and sweeping
07

Labor & Equipment

Crews and operated iron on time and material when the job needs hands and machines rather than a fixed scope. Tell us how many men and what machine, and we'll put them on your site.

  • Skilled labor crews on time and material
  • Compressor crews — one or two men with jackhammers and chipping guns
  • Operated equipment, machine and operator together
  • Backhoe work — trenching, footings, loading and site prep
  • Loader, breaker and grapple work
  • Planer, sweeper, auger and trencher attachments
  • Truck and tools
  • Prevailing wage operators and laborers for public works
  • Nights, weekends and overtime
