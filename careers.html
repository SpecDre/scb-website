Careers

We're hiring

Steady work out of one yard. If you can run a saw, a skid steer or a truck, price a job or keep the schedule straight — or you're willing to learn — come talk to us.

Open positions

What we're looking for

Most of our crews run cutting, coring, grinding, demo, dirt and hauling — often all of it on the same job. Experience helps, but showing up on time and working safe matters more. We hire in the office too — estimating and dispatch.

01

Saw Operator

Flat saw, wall saw and hand saw work. Slab, wall and deck cutting on commercial jobs across Southern California.

  • Experience preferred, will train the right person
  • Valid driver's license
  • Able to work nights and weekends when the job calls for it
02

Skid Steer Operator

Bobcat and mini excavator work — breaking, grinding, grading, trenching and loading in tight urban footprints.

  • Prior equipment experience required
  • Comfortable with attachments — breaker, planer, auger, sweeper
  • Valid driver's license
03

Core Drill Operator

Slab, wall, overhead and angled coring for plumbing, electrical, HVAC and anchor work.

  • Experience preferred, will train
  • Attention to layout and dimensions
  • Valid driver's license
04

Laborer

Demolition, cleanup, compressor crew work and general support. Good entry point into the trade.

  • No experience necessary
  • Reliable transportation
  • Able to do physical work in all conditions
05

Truck Driver

Hauling concrete, asphalt, dirt and spoils off our jobs and moving equipment between sites.

  • Class A or B license
  • Clean driving record
  • Dump truck experience preferred
06

Estimator

Reading plans, running takeoffs and pricing cutting, coring, demo and dirt work for general contractors bidding commercial jobs.

  • Concrete cutting or demolition estimating experience preferred
  • Able to read plans and do quantity takeoffs
  • Comfortable talking scope through with a PM or super
  • Office based, out of the yard
07

Dispatcher

Scheduling crews, trucks and equipment across jobs, and taking the calls when a GC needs somebody on site tonight.

  • Dispatch or construction scheduling experience preferred
  • Level headed when the schedule changes mid-day
  • Rotating after-hours and weekend coverage
  • Office based, out of the yard
08
