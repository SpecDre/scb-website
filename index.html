What we do

One sub for the whole scope

Most of our work comes in as a subcontract from general contractors who need an opening cut, a lot ground down, a slab gone, a footprint cleared, and the debris off the site before the next trade shows up. We do all of it with our own crews, so there's one contract, one schedule and one number to call when something changes, including nights and weekends.

01

Concrete Cutting

Clean, precise cuts in slab, wall and deck with controlled slurry and minimal impact on the surrounding structure.

  • Flat sawing
  • Wall sawing
  • Wire sawing
  • Hand sawing & curb cutting
02

Core Drilling

Cored openings for plumbing, electrical, HVAC and anchor work — overhead, angled and through post-tension slabs.

  • Slab & wall coring
  • Overhead coring
  • Angle & deep coring
  • Diameters: 1/4 in. through 60 in.
03

Asphalt & Concrete Grinding

Skid steer mounted asphalt planers for trip hazards, high spots, transitions and surface prep — no tear-out and repave.

  • Asphalt planing & cold milling
  • Trip hazard removal
  • Transition & high spot grinding
  • Surface prep before overlay
04

Demolition & Breaking

Selective interior demo and structural breaking with compressor crews and breakers sized to the space and the schedule.

  • Selective interior demolition
  • Tenant improvement demo
  • Compressor crews & jackhammers
  • Breaking & busting
  • Skid steer breaking
05

Excavation & Grading

Dirt work with Bobcat and mini excavator — trenching, footings, pads and site prep in tight urban footprints.

  • Trenching & footings
  • Grading & site prep
  • Auger & post holes
  • Mini excavator work
06

Hauling & Removal

We haul our own spoils, so the material doesn't sit on your job.

  • Concrete & asphalt removal
  • Dirt & spoils hauling
  • Dump truck service
  • Site cleanup

Common questions

What contractors ask before they hire us

I'm in a bind — can you get out here fast?

Call dispatch. Nights, weekends and holidays included. Our equipment is in our own yard, so we can move on short notice.

Do you work nights and weekends?

Regularly. Occupied buildings, active retail, live hospitals and tight schedules often can't take daytime work. Tell us the window when you send the scope and we'll price it that way.

Do you grind asphalt?

Yes — skid steer mounted asphalt planers for trip hazards, high spots, transitions, ADA corrections and surface prep before an overlay. The planer cuts to grade in a single pass, and it's usually a fraction of the cost of tearing out and repaving.

Do you haul your own debris?

Yes. Hauling and removal are in house, so the concrete, asphalt and spoils leave with our crew.

Do you carry insurance and provide certificates?

Yes. We're fully licensed and insured, and we send certificates and additional insured endorsements for your project file on request.

How do I get a bid?

Send plans, a scope sheet, or just a description of what needs to come out. Email the estimating desk or use the form on the contact page and we'll get a number back to you.

What areas do you serve?

We work throughout Southern California out of our yard. If you're not sure whether your job is in range, call and ask.
